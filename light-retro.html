About

Who is Anis Cheriet?

I'm an entrepreneur who builds things from scratch. Not decks. Not strategies. Real products, real infrastructure, real businesses.

I studied entrepreneurship, then went straight into the field. I deployed EV charging networks across Europe — figuring out logistics, regulations, and operations in multiple countries. I've lived and worked in different countries, which taught me how to adapt fast and think globally.

I've built mobile apps, explored AI applied to electric transportation, and launched hardware prototypes with small teams. I don't wait for the perfect plan — I ship, learn, and iterate.

Right now, I'm on a new chapter: building one project at a time, in public. Every day is a new challenge. Follow the journey below.
